🥗 Vitamins

Vitamins are essential micronutrients that help your body function properly. They support growth, immunity, and overall health.

Key Vitamins

  • Vitamin A: Supports vision and immune function. Found in carrots, sweet potatoes, spinach.
  • Vitamin B: Helps with energy production. Found in whole grains, eggs, meat.
  • Vitamin C: Boosts immunity. Found in citrus fruits, berries, bell peppers.
  • Vitamin D: Supports bone health. Found in fortified milk, eggs, sunlight exposure.

Daily Requirements

Vitamin Age group (4-8 yrs) Age group (9-18 yrs)
Vitamin A 400 µg 600-900 µg
Vitamin C 25 mg 45-75 mg
Vitamin D 600 IU 600-800 IU
{% elif category == 'proteins' %}

🥩 Proteins

Proteins are the building blocks of your body. They help build muscles, repair tissues, and make enzymes and hormones.

Protein Sources

Animal Proteins

  • Chicken & Turkey
  • Fish & Seafood
  • Eggs
  • Milk & Yogurt
  • Cheese

Plant Proteins

  • Lentils & Beans
  • Chickpeas
  • Nuts & Seeds
  • Tofu & Tempeh
  • Whole Grains

Daily Protein Requirements

Children (4-8 years): 19-25g per day

Teens (9-18 years): 34-52g per day

Adults (example): 46-56g per day (depending on sex and activity)

{% elif category == 'minerals' %}

⚡ Minerals

Minerals like calcium, iron, and zinc are vital for bone health, oxygen transport, and immune function.

Essential Minerals

Calcium

Builds strong bones and teeth

Sources: Milk, cheese, yogurt, leafy greens

Daily need: 1000-1300mg

Iron

Carries oxygen in blood

Sources: Lean meat, eggs, lentils, spinach

Daily need: 8-15mg

Zinc

Supports immunity and growth

Sources: Meat, shellfish, legumes, seeds

Daily need: 5-11mg
