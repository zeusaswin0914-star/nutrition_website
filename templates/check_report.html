How It Works

  1. Upload: Select the age category and upload the blood report image/PDF.
  2. Analysis: Our system extracts and analyzes key biomarkers (hemoglobin, iron, vitamins, etc.).
  3. Recommendations: Receive personalized nutrition recommendations based on age and blood values.
  4. Diet Plan: Get a customized meal plan to address any nutritional deficiencies.
  5. Report: Download a comprehensive PDF report with all recommendations.
Key Biomarkers We Analyze

Hemoglobin & Iron

Indicates oxygen-carrying capacity and anemia risk. Low values suggest need for iron-rich foods.

Normal: 12-16 g/dL (varies by age/sex)

Vitamin D

Essential for bone health and calcium absorption. Deficiency common in children.

Normal: 30-100 ng/mL

Calcium & Phosphorus

Critical for bone strength, especially in growing children and aging adults.

Normal: 8.5-10.2 mg/dL (calcium)

Protein (Albumin)

Indicates protein status and overall nutritional health.

Normal: 3.5-5.5 g/dL

Blood Sugar (Glucose)

Fasting glucose indicates energy metabolism and diabetes risk.

Normal: 70-100 mg/dL (fasting)

Cholesterol Profile

Total, LDL, HDL cholesterol — important for heart health at any age.

Healthy: Total <200 mg/dL

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upload a report for someone else?

Yes! You can analyze any family member's blood report — child, teen, adult, or senior. Just select their age category.

What if I don't have a blood report?

You can still use our general diet plans by age category. For personalized recommendations, a blood report from a recent lab test is ideal.

How long does analysis take?

Usually a few seconds to a minute. You'll receive instant recommendations and can download a PDF report.

Is my data private?

All uploaded reports are stored securely. We do not share personal health information with third parties.

Can I compare multiple reports?

Yes! Upload and save multiple reports to track progress and nutrition changes over time.
